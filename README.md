# GoTool Center

## What is GoTool Center?

### 1. Gotool Center is a toolchain extension for Godot Engine that aims to provide additional functionality for development workflow. Gotool provides the following:
   * __Project scanning__ into an sqlite3 database to ensure the extension maintains high level performance.
   * __Project analysis__ to identify where problems may arise, where best practices are not followed, where dependancies are non-functional, and more.
   * __JSON tooling__ to assist in schema changes, automatic conversion to SQL/SQLite3/ConfigFile from JSON, migration, and more.
   * __Export Automation__ to common platforms to remove pain points during the process.
   * __Asset Management__ to identify unused, misplaced, oversized, duplicated or suspicious assets.
   * __Diagnostic__ tooling for identifying broken references, missing files, invalid paths, inconsistencies in scripts, missing imports, and more.
   * __GitHub Integration__ to allow you to work from a repository directly from GoTool Center.
### 2. GoTool Center is an open source, MIT permissive project that allows community inclusion in development, issue reporting, security concerns, discussions, and more.
### 3. GoTool Center puts theme, configuration, and features in your hands through extensive and expansive customization options.
